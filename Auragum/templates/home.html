Why AuraGum™

Most chewing gums on the market are made with plastic-based ingredients that never break down — polluting sidewalks, oceans, and landfills. AuraGum™ is different.

We designed our gum to be fully biodegradable, using natural chicle instead of synthetic bases. Our packaging is minimal, compostable, and plastic-free from start to finish.

But we didn’t stop at sustainability. AuraGum™ is also functional — with options for energy, hydration, or just fresh breath. Clean ingredients, clean impact, clean gum.
